WHAT AFFECTS WATER CONSUMPTION THE MOST?
----------------------------------------

* time of day (hours)

* season (group of months)

* humidity (weather.humidity)

* temperature (weather.temperature)

* cloud cover (weather.cloud_cover)

* rain (weather.precip_intensity AND weather.precip_probability)

* power usage (power.hourly_kwh)

* solar energy production -- correlates to sun (power.hourly_solar_kWh)
